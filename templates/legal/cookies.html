1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

TrendWatcher uses cookies for:

  • Essential functionality: Remember your preferences (dark mode, language)
  • Analytics: Understand how visitors use the site (with your consent)
  • Marketing: Track affiliate link clicks (with your consent)

4. Belgian Law & Cookie Consent

Important: Under Belgian law, we cannot require you to accept cookies to use TrendWatcher. You can reject all non-essential cookies and still access the full site.

We respect your choice. When you first visit, a cookie banner will appear with options to:

  • Accept all: Enable all cookies
  • Reject all: Only essential cookies
  • Customize: Choose per category

5. Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can change your cookie settings at any time:

  • On TrendWatcher: Click "Cookie Preferences" in the footer
  • In your browser: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies in settings

6. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by third-party services we use:

  • Google Analytics: Privacy Policy
  • Amazon Associates: Privacy Policy

We have no control over third-party cookies. Please review their privacy policies.

7. Do Not Track (DNT)

We respect "Do Not Track" browser signals. If DNT is enabled, we will not load analytics or marketing cookies automatically.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated "Last updated" date.
