Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 9, 2025

1. Who We Are

TrendWatcher is a news aggregation and trending insights platform operated from Brussels, Belgium. We collect and display trending news from various sources to help you stay informed.

Contact: info@trendwatcher.be

2. What Data We Collect

We collect minimal personal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Email address: When you subscribe to our newsletter
  • IP address (hashed): For security and fraud prevention
  • Click data: When you click affiliate links (anonymized)
  • Cookies: For analytics and preferences (see Cookie Policy)

3. Why We Collect This Data

  • Newsletter: To send you weekly trend digests (with your consent)
  • Analytics: To improve our service and understand user behavior
  • Affiliate tracking: To measure effectiveness of product recommendations
  • Security: To prevent spam and abuse

4. Legal Basis (GDPR Art. 6)

We process your data based on:

  • Consent: Newsletter subscriptions, analytics cookies
  • Legitimate interest: Security, fraud prevention, service improvement

5. How Long We Keep Your Data

  • Newsletter subscribers: Until you unsubscribe + 3 years (legal requirement)
  • Click logs: 12 months, then anonymized
  • Consent records: 3 years after withdrawal

6. Who Has Access to Your Data

We share minimal data with trusted third parties:

  • Render.com: Hosting provider (USA, EU-US Data Privacy Framework compliant)
  • NewsData.io: News API provider (GDPR compliant)
  • Google Analytics: Analytics (only with your consent)

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

7. Your Rights (GDPR)

You have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a copy of your data
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to object: Stop processing for marketing purposes
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in machine-readable format
  • Right to withdraw consent: Unsubscribe from newsletter anytime

To exercise your rights: Email us at privacy@trendwatcher.be

8. Data Security

We protect your data with:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Secure, encrypted database storage
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and logging

9. Data Breaches

In case of a data breach affecting your personal data, we will notify you and the Belgian Data Protection Authority within 72 hours as required by GDPR Art. 33.

Belgian DPA: gegevensbeschermingsautoriteit.be

10. Children's Privacy

TrendWatcher is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. For significant changes, we will notify subscribers via email.

12. Contact & Complaints

Email: privacy@trendwatcher.be
Address: TrendWatcher, Brussels, Belgium
